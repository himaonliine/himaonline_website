12ft.io

"Show me a 10ft paywalls, I'll show you a 12ft ladder". Adding 12ft.io to the beginning of any URL will allow you to read or share an article. You can also type a URL directly into their website.



Kemono

Fuck paying for Patreon and Gumroad, just get your fix here! Mostly NSFW but there are some SFW stuff here. Just look up your favorite creator on here!



scribe.rip

Like 12ft, Scribe is a proxy which serves unpaywalled content but only from the website Medium. This means you can replace "medium.com" or the custom domain of any Medium blog with "scribe.rip" and view the content.



Sci-Hub

SciHub contains 85 million unlocked scholarly articles, but it only works if you give it a specific URL (usually a DOI or a citation), and that document is available in their database.
